3. advanced find method
형식: db.collection.find({age : {$gt: 8}})
$eq = equal
$gt = greater than
$gte = greater than and equal
$in = in an array
$lt = less than
$lte = les than and equal
$ne = not equal
$nin = not in array

$in method 
db.collection.find({breed: {$in: ['Mutt', 'Corgi']}})
breed라는 property에 배열안에 있는 값이 값으로 설정되어 있는 data값들을 불러오는 것
